.           METHODS            .
.                              .
*------------------------------*


==> add     --> This immediately adds `elt` to the scene.

==> play    --> This animates the creation of `elt` step-by-step, gradually drawing it on the screen.

==> scale   --> This resizes `elt` by a specified factor.

==> rotate  --> This rotates `elt` by a specified angle (in radians), turning it around its center point.
